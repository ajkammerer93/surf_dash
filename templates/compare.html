A Free Surfline Alternative

Full 7-day forecasts, buoys, tides, and cams. No account, no ads, no paywall.

If you landed here, you're probably looking for a surf forecast that doesn't cap your surf checks, lock cameras behind a subscription, or make you create an account to see tomorrow's swell. That's exactly why Free Surf Forecast exists: every feature is free, for every visitor, with no sign-up — the way MagicSeaweed used to work.

What You Give Up

Honesty matters more than a sales pitch. Surfline has the largest surf camera network in the world, human forecasters writing regional analysis, a proprietary wave model tuned per spot, and global coverage. If you need cams at a specific break we don't cover, or 16-day outlooks for a surf trip abroad, Surfline Premium earns its price.

What You Get

  • The same government wave models professionals start from — NOAA GFS-Wave, WaveWatch III — presented raw, with transparent scoring on top
  • Real-time NDBC and CDIP buoy observations, including wave energy spectra
  • Swell-direction-aware spot scoring that accounts for each beach's facing direction
  • A session planner that scores every daylight 3-hour window for your skill level
  • Live swell maps: ocean-basin overview plus local wave height, period, and wind grids
  • Push alerts when conditions at your spot turn good — no email address required
  • An installable app (PWA) that works on your phone's home screen

No account. No limit on how many times you check it. If it saves you a skunked session, it did its job.
